
# Virtual Private Network (VPN)

**Source:** [Gate Smashers](https://youtu.be/JFXBjlT5cGU?si=QhurxkhIhuMr2OD5)
**Topic:** Network Security

---

## 1. What is VPN?
* **VPN (Virtual Private Network)** is a service that protects your internet connection and privacy online.
* It creates an encrypted tunnel for your data, protects your online identity by hiding your IP address, and allows you to use public Wi-Fi hotspots safely.

## 2. Why Use VPN? (Real-Life Examples)
* **Public Wi-Fi Safety:** Protects data when using insecure networks at airports, cafes, or stations.
* **Work From Home:** Allows remote employees to securely access internal company servers without needing expensive physical "Leased Lines."

## 3. How It Works (The "Tunneling" Concept)
1.  **Encryption:** The VPN software on your device encrypts your data **before** it leaves your computer.
2.  **Tunneling:** It encapsulates the data packet inside a secure "wrapper."
    * **Data** (Content) is hidden.
    * **Metadata** (IP address, Location) is masked.
3.  **Transmission:** The ISP or hackers can only see the "wrapper" (encrypted traffic) but cannot read the content inside.
4.  **Decryption:** The VPN Server at the other end has the key to decrypt the data and forward it to the destination.

## 4. Key Differences

| Feature | Without VPN | With VPN |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Visibility** | Visible to ISP & Hackers | Encrypted & Unreadable |
| **IP Address** | Your Real IP is exposed | Real IP is hidden (Masked) |
| **Security** | Low (Open Network) | High (Virtual Private Tunnel) |
| **Cost** | Low (Public Internet) | Low (Uses Public Internet) vs High (Leased Line) |
